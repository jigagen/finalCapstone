Renewable Technology and Permaculture

"At present, we are stealing the future, selling it in the present, and calling it gross domestic product"
Paul Hawken

Globally, around 8 million people die each year from pollution caused by the burning of fossil fuels. Also the average price for petrol has gone up by 48% which is making it more unaffordable for the average person. Renewable technology is going to help us reduce our reliance on fossil fuels. There are some cons to using renewable tech as anything that needs to be mass produced will have an impact on the environment. Permaculture is an ecological design system which seeks to create sustainable human settlements and agricultural systems modeled from natural ecosystems. It is based on the principle of working with, rather than against nature.
